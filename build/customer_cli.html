lastname (str): Lastname in alphabetical value.

email (str): Email in alphabetical value and in form alpha@alpha.alpha.

phone (str): Phone number in numerical value.

company (str): Customer’s company in alphabetical value.

–email (str): For change: Email in alphabetical value and in form alpha@alpha.alpha, for keep unchanged: None.

—phone (str): For change: Phone number in numerical value, for keep unchanged: None.
